Computer networking and the internet are fundamental components of modern technology and communication.

#. Computer Networking:

1. Definition: Computer networking refers to the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share resources and communicate with each other.

2. Types of Networks:

* PAN (Personal Area Network): A interconnects technology devices, typically within the range of a single user, which is approximately 10 meters or 30 feet.

* LAN (Local Area Network): A network confined to a small geographic area, like an office, building, or campus.

* WAN (Wide Area Network): It’s Spans a large geographic area, often connecting multiple LANs.

* MAN (Metropolitan Area Network): It’s Covers a city or large Area.

3. Network-topology:

There are six Network-topology

* Point-to-point

* Bus topology

* Star topology

* Ring topology

* Tree topology

* Mesh topology

4. Advantage & Disadvantege

ADVANTAGES

* Resource sharing

* Sharing files

* Fast data transfer

* Easy to take backup

DISADVANTAGES

* Additional cost of networking equipment

* Management of large network

* Health issues

* Security risks
